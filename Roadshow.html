Innovation and Creativity

Roadmap

To commemorate World Innovation and Creativity Day, an exciting island-wide roadshow will bring energy, imagination and inspiration directly to our students. The highlight of the day will be the official introduction of “Sparky”, our vibrant innovation mascot, who will visit schools across Saint Lucia, spending up to 30 minutes at each location to engage students in fun, creative activities and short motivational interactions.
